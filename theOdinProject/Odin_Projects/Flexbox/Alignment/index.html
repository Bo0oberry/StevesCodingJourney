Knowledge Check

What is the difference between justify-content and align-items?
  • justify-content: aligns items on the main-axis
  • align-items: aligns itmes on the cross-axis
How do you use flexbox to completely center a div inside a flex container?
  • justify-content: center
  • align-items: center
What’s the difference between justify-content: space-between and justify-content: space-around?
  • space-between: items are placed apart from each other and equal distance
  • space-around: items are places apart from each other and the container by giving them each an equal left and right "margin" based on the availible space left
